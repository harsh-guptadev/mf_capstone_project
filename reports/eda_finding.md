# EDA Findings

1. NAV values showed a strong upward trend during 2023, indicating a broad market bull run.

2. Several schemes experienced temporary NAV declines during 2024, reflecting market corrections.

3. SBI Mutual Fund maintained the highest AUM among all fund houses.

4. SIP inflows increased consistently from 2022 to 2025, reaching record highs in late 2025.

5. Liquid funds attracted the highest net inflows among all categories.

6. Equity folios contributed the largest share of total investor folios.

7. Maharashtra, Karnataka, Delhi and Telangana accounted for a significant share of investor transactions.

8. B30 cities contributed a meaningful share of investments, showing growing participation beyond metro markets.

9. Banking, IT, Pharma and Financial Services were among the largest portfolio sectors.

10. Most equity-oriented schemes exhibited positive return correlations, indicating common market exposure.
